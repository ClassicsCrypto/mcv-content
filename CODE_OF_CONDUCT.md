# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
open-content-engine community a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of
background or identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, friendly,
and healthy community.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its code of conduct. The Covenant defines, in full, the expected behavior, the enforcement
responsibilities of maintainers, the scope, and the enforcement guidelines for this community.
Contributors and maintainers are expected to uphold it.

We adopt the Covenant **by reference** rather than reproducing its text here; please read the
canonical version at the link above. Any future revision we adopt will update the version named in
this file.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository's issues, pull requests,
discussions, and commit history — and whenever an individual is officially representing the project
in a public space.

## Enforcement

Concerns may be reported privately to the maintainers at **jayb@marscatsvoyage.com** (the same
private channel described in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md)). Reports will be reviewed promptly and
fairly, and maintainers will respect the privacy and security of anyone who reports an incident.
