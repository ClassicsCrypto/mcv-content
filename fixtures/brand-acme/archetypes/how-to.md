## When to use

Onboarding moments, recurring friction points (alignment, focus, collimation), or seasonal
care. This is the brand's strongest lane — every claim is verifiable at the eyepiece.

## Structure

1. **Hook** — name the task and the payoff ("a sharp Moon in three minutes").
2. **Steps** — short, numbered, in order, no skipped assumptions.
3. **Common miss** — the one thing people get wrong, named plainly.
4. **Close** — what "done right" looks like.

- "Cold night? Let the scope sit outside for 20 minutes before you expect a crisp image."
